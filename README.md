# RENEW'D

RENEW'D is a match-based shopping experience that lets users refresh their wardrobe without spending any money!


### Buying new clothes can be expensive. 

RENEW'D solves this by turning your closet into currency

⭐ By matching people through style, sizing, and location, RENEW'D keeps fashion circular – reducing waste and expanding your wardrobe 
⭐ RENEW'D reimagines thrifting as an effortless experience that can now be personalized
⭐ Users can match with items that fit their criteria in exchange for pieces from their own closets

A world of preloved items now at your fingertips!
